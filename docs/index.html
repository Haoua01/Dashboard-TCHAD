About This Project

This project aims to provide a comprehensive mapping of banking and non-banking equipment in the CEMAC region. We focus on financial inclusion in Chad and seek to model the relationship between access to these facilities and the economic development of the region.

Our Services

We offer insights into the various financial service providers and their roles in enhancing financial inclusion in the region. Our research is designed to provide actionable data for stakeholders.
